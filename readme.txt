Observational study considering how the price of a full-unit AirBnB listing in Portland, OR varies based on its distance from Pioneer Courthouse Square, accounting for the number of bedrooms and bathrooms.

I discovered that, with 95% confidence, a 1 meter increase of a full-unit AirBnB listing's distance from Pioneer Courthouse Square is associated with between a $0.0000518 decrease and a $0.0000168 increase in the price of the listing, when accounting for the number of bedrooms and bathrooms.

AirBnB data sourced from https://insideairbnb.com/
